52 Card Shoot'em

This is my most recent game. It's inspired by one of my favourite roguelikes 'Brotato'.
Here are some of the highlight features:

  • Multiple waves of enemies that scale in difficulty
  • A Black Jack card system which rewards the player for getting as close to 21 as possible
  • A shop of randomly selected items that upgrade you character between waves
  • A levelling up system
