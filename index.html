Who am I?

Hi, I'm Frank, a games engineering student at Newcastle University. The things I love about creating computer games
are the uniqueness and variety of the medium and the sense that you’re really working towards an end goal during the
creation process. Although I’d dabbled in coding, my interest in pursuing a career in the games industry was fully
ignited during my A Level programming project. I found myself genuinely excited about working on it and was able to
make something I was really proud of. Most of my games engineering experience so far has been working with the Unity
games engine and programming in C#, but I’d be more than happy to learn other languages and engines in preparation for
and during my industry placement.
