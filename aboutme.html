Who am I?

Hi, I'm Frank, a games engineering student at Newcastle University. What makes me passionate about games is the
uniqueness and variety of the medium and the sense that you're really working towards something during their
creation. While I had been dabbling in games engineering in my earlier years, I chose to pursue a career in the
industry after a programming project in Sixth Form which I found myself genuinely passionate and excited about,
something I hadn't really experienced in my other subjects. Most of my games engineering experience so far has been
working with the Unity games engine and programming in C#, but I'm happy to learn other languages and engines in
preparation for and during my industry placement.
